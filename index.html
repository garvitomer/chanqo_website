FAQs

Is it safe to trade on Chanqo?

How does Chanqo Works?

Select the event you would like to trade. Choose 'Yes' or 'No' and number of contracts you would like to buy. Chanqo will then match you with another players that has opposite view.

Maximum Limit per month?

How to withdraw Money?

What is a contract?

A contract gives players the ability to make trades on their opinions about an event. Each contract has two options 'Yes' and 'No'. After expiry every event will settle to either 'Yes' or 'No'. You will get ₹ 1 for every correctly answered contract and ₹ 0 for wrong one.

Any Example?

Sure. Let's say you think India GDP will be more than 8% for 2022-23 and you bought 200 'Yes' contract @ ₹ 0.4. i.e. Total value of ₹ 80. If actual GDP comes greater than 8%, then this contract will be settled at 'Yes'. As you had 200 bought 'Yes' contract you will get ₹ 1 for each contract i.e. ₹ 200. And your gross profit in this trade will be ₹ (200 - 80) = ₹ 120.

I have bought one contract of "Yes" and "No", why I have no position?

Each contract has two option either "Yes" or "No". So, if you buy both then effectively, you have no position. You can calculate your profit as ₹ (1-"Yes" buy price + "No" buy price).

Who determines contract price and why does it change?

All the players on Chanqo effectively determine the price of any contract. So, if lot of participants think that event will settle at 'Yes', then the price of "Yes" will increase and "No" will decrease.

What is an Order and its types?

When you click to buy a contract you basically place a working order. Chanqo will try to match you with other participants that has opposite view. You can cancel your order at anytime by clicking on it and select 'Cancel'.

Why money is deducted everytime I place an Order?

Chanqo treat every order as different and will deduct the total value of that order from your wallet. And when a position is closed, it will return all the money + net profit/ loss made. For Example, if you buy 100 'Yes' @ 0.7, then ₹ 70 (100x0.7) will be deducted from your wallet. Let's say you place sell order of 100 'Yes' @ 0.8. Then, Chanqo will deduct ₹ 80 (100x0.8) again as it treats every order differently. And as soon as you get filled your net position will be 0. So it will return ₹ 160 i.e. 100x0.7 + 100x0.8+ 100x(0.8-0.7) [Profit]

How to add money to wallet and how much time will it take?

Cancel & Refund Policy of Chanqo


1. Below is the policy in relation to the Cancellation and Refund of the Chanqo App and www.chanqo.com (“Chanqo App”)

2. Once a trade has been placed on the Chanqo App and has been matched with another user, the same cannot be cancelled.

3. In case of the event being cancelled/abandoned, the trade will be automatically cancelled and the money will be refunded to the wallet.

4. In case there is no determinable result before the event expiry date, the event will be treated as cancelled and the money will be refunded to the wallet.

5. However, apart from the instances set out in this policy, refund of no other nature will be allowed.

6. Our decision on refunds shall be at our sole discretion and shall be final and binding.
